With the key ingredient of bananas, you can whip up a delicious and simple **Vegan Banana Smoothie**. This recipe is not only refreshing but also vegan-friendly and healthy. Here’s how to make it:

### Vegan Banana Smoothie Recipe

#### Ingredients:
- 2 ripe bananas
- 1 cup almond milk (or any plant-based milk)
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional for added nutrition)
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Ice cubes (optional for a colder drink)

#### Instructions:

1. **Prepare Ingredients**:
- Peel the bananas.
- If you’re using chia seeds, soak them in a little water for about 10 minutes until they become gel-like. This step is optional.

2. **Blend the Smoothie**:
- In a blender, combine the peeled bananas, almond milk, chia seeds (if using), vanilla extract, and a handful of ice cubes if you prefer a chilled smoothie.
- Blend on high until the mixture is smooth and creamy.

3. **Serve**:
- Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately for maximum freshness and flavor.

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes
#### Total Time: 5 minutes

This Vegan Banana Smoothie is perfect as a quick breakfast or a refreshing afternoon snack.
